CommuniTech

Outreach Chair

CommuniTech is a community service organization at the University of Pennsylvania aimed to increase exposure to technology in low-income communities. As a group we volunteer to teach students concepts about computers and make donations of used hardware to schools requesting technology for classrooms/extracurriculars/etc. As the Outreach chair, I make public the recent activities of the organization (i.e. post about recent donations, advertise service projects, etc).

Science and Technology Wing (STWing)

The Science and Technology Wing is a collection of several hundred undergraduates, graduate students, professors, and alumni at the University of Pennsylvania

Penn Aerial Robotics

The mission of Penn AiR is to engage undergraduate students of the University of Pennsylvania in the development of aerial robots, UAVs and other unmanned vehicles. The club aims to do so by providing students with the resources to construct, develop and test their own robots, travel to and compete at inter-collegiate competitions, and to connect with people who share this same passion.

South Asia Society

The University of Pennsylvania South Asia Society (SAS) is a student organization dedicated to promoting cultural unity, social interaction, and political awareness relating to the South Asian community at Penn. Specifically SAS goals are to increase awareness and educate the Penn community of the various cultures and heritages represented in the South Asian subcontinent.
